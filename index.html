STREET FIGHTER GAME

Frogger is a classic arcade game released by Konami in 1981. The game involves guiding a frog across a busy road and river to reach its home on the other side. It became a massive success and has since become a beloved classic, inspiring numerous imitators and spin-offs, and has been referenced and parodied in popular culture..

Frogger's simple yet addictive gameplay, charming graphics, and catchy soundtrack made it an instant hit. It quickly became one of the most popular arcade games of the 1980s and has since been ported to numerous home consoles and computers.
